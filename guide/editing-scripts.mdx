# Edit and Refine Your Script

Once your video idea has been generated, you can easily **edit**, **refine**, or **reimagine** it within Tabario.  
This step gives you full creative control before producing your final video.

---

## Why Edit or Refine?

AI can generate great starting points, but your brand’s voice and goals are unique.  
The **Edit and Refine** feature helps you make sure your video aligns perfectly with your style, audience, and message.

---

## Editing Options

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your generated idea">
    After your idea has been created, click **“Edit Idea”** to access the editing panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manually edit the script">
    You can freely modify the text to better fit your message, tone, or brand identity.  
    Edits are instantly saved so you can preview your updates anytime.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate variations">
    Not satisfied with the first version? Click **“Generate Variation”** to let the AI propose alternative phrasings or styles while keeping your original intent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change tone of voice">
    Transform your script’s style by selecting a new tone — for example:
    - Professional  
    - Funny  
    - Friendly  
    - Inspirational  
    - Serious  
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Best Practices

<Card title="Tips for Effective Refinement" icon="lightbulb">
- Keep your script concise and engaging — aim for **30–60 seconds** of spoken content.  
- Test different tones to see what fits your audience best.  
- Make sure your call-to-action (CTA) is clear and placed near the end of the script.  
- Use natural, conversational language for better AI voiceover flow.
</Card>

Experiment to find the perfect balance of creativity and clarity.
