# Generate Videos with AI

You’ve created your idea, refined your script, and chosen a style — now it’s time to bring your vision to life.  
The **Generate Videos with AI** step transforms your concept into a fully produced, ready-to-share video.

---

## Overview

Tabario uses advanced AI to automatically:
- Combine your script with visuals that match your chosen art style  
- Generate natural-sounding voiceovers  
- Add subtitles in the same language as the voice  
- Produce a complete video optimized for social media platforms  

This process typically takes **10–30 minutes**, depending on the script length.

---

## How to Generate Your Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Generate Video screen">
    After selecting your video style, click **“Generate Video”** to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your voice">
    Choose from available AI-generated voices that fit your tone and message.  
    Voices vary in gender, pitch, and style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set subtitle language">
    The subtitles are automatically generated in the same language as the selected voice.  
    *(Supported languages: English (US) and Brazilian Portuguese (PT-BR))*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and generate">
    Review your selections, then click **“Generate”**.  
    The AI will process your video and notify you once it’s ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Processing Time

<Tip>
Video generation takes between **10–30 minutes**, depending on script length.  
Currently, Tabario supports videos of up to **60 seconds** in duration.
</Tip>

---

## Customization Options

<Card title="During generation" icon="gear">
- You can return later to your project list to view the status.  
- Generation continues in the background even if you close the app.  
- Once complete, your video will appear in the “My Videos” section for playback or download.
</Card>

---

## Best Practices for Great Results

<Card title="Recommendations" icon="lightbulb">
- Keep your script concise — aim for 30 to 60 seconds.  
- Match your **voice** and **visual style** with your audience (e.g., professional voice for cinematic, playful tone for crayon or anime).  
- Avoid overly complex narration with too many scene changes — simple messages produce the best pacing and visuals.
</Card>

---

## Example Workflow

1. Generate an idea: *“A short inspirational ad about staying consistent in fitness (English)”*  
2. Refine and set tone: *Motivational and empowering*  
3. Choose style: *Cinematic*  
4. Voice: *Warm male narrator, English (US)*  
5. Generate → Wait 20 minutes → Video ready for sharing
