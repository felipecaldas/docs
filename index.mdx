# Welcome to Tabario

**Tabario** helps creators, marketers, and agencies generate social media videos quickly and easily using the power of AI.  
No editing experience required — just describe your idea, and Tabario brings it to life.

## Key Features

- Fast video generation (10–30 minutes)  
- AI-driven creativity from idea to production  
- Multilingual support: English (US) and Brazilian Portuguese  
- Customizable tones, voices, and visual styles  
- Supports up to 60-second video length
